Welcome to Our School

Welcome to Daisies Academy, a co-educational private institution established in the 2009/2010 academic session and certified by the Education Department of the Federal Capital Territory Administration, Abuja.


We comprise Early Years, Primary and Secondary School, with a vision of “Laying a Solid Foundation.” Our school has maintained high academic standards since inception, producing pupils who gain admission and scholarships into top private colleges in Abuja.


Mrs Onyinye Christopher
Headteacher

Our School Sections

Nursery

Childcare and crèche for under 4 years. DISCOVERIES 1 & 2 for ages 2–4 and FOUNDATION for ages 4–5, preparing pupils for primary education.

Primary

Years Group 1–5 for children aged 5–9 years, exposing pupils to higher learning using materials a year ahead of their class.

Secondary

Years 7 to 12, offering a broad curriculum and preparation for BECE, WAEC, SAT, Cambridge and NECO examinations.
